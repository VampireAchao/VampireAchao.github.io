![image-20210704224107780](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20210704224107780.png)

登录后刷新一下页面

![image-20210704224123834](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20210704224123834.png)

选择想要的样式，复制下面的代码

<a target="_blank" href="http://wpa.qq.com/msgrd?v=3&uin=1441470436&site=qq&menu=yes"><img border="0" src="http://wpa.qq.com/pa?p=2:1441470436:51" alt="点击这里给我发消息" title="点击这里给我发消息"/></a>
