有时我们会需要统计代码行数，可以使用`idea`的插件`statistic`

[下载地址](https://plugins.jetbrains.com/plugin/4509-statistic)

![image-20201218195145400](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20201218195145400.png)

下载后安装

![image-20201218195413114](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20201218195413114.png)

安装完成点击确定，然后重启`idea`

点击`Statistic`的`Refresh`

![image-20201218195513981](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20201218195513981.png)

可以看到已经统计完成

![image-20201218195655478](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20201218195655478.png)
