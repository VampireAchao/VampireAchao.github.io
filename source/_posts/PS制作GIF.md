昨天写了篇博客，是关于`CSS`实现图片旋转的，群里的小伙伴跟我说博客里看不到效果让我做个`GIF`。。。那我今天就来写篇如何用`PhotoShop`制作`GIF`

我们先把图片拖入`ps`

![image-20200826220603774](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20200826220603774.png)

![image-20200826220622084](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20200826220622084.png)

然后解锁背景图层

![image-20200826220728040](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20200826220728040.png)

然后选中图层

![image-20200826220821350](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20200826220821350.png)

`Ctrl+J`复制图层

![image-20200826221113042](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20200826221113042.png)

然后给每个图层调整角度旋转。。。

选中图层然后按Ctrl+T

![image-20200826221214718](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20200826221214718.png)

然后拖动

![image-20200826221307773](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20200826221307773.png)

重复步骤

![image-20200826221802134](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20200826221802134.png)

然后点窗口`->`时间轴

![image-20200826221915493](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20200826221915493.png)

然后点击从图层建立帧

![image-20200826222057599](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20200826222057599.png)

调整时间

![image-20200826222152128](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20200826222152128.png)

文件`->`存储为`web`所用格式

![image-20200826222243822](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20200826222243822.png)

选择`gif`

![image-20200826222311884](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20200826222311884.png)

点击存储

![image-20200826222334682](/imgs/oss/picGo/image-20200826222334682.png)

然后就做好了
