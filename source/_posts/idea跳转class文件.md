![image-20230113230258854](/imgs/oss/blog/vampireachao/image-20230113230258854.png)

```shell
idea64
$FileNameWithoutExtension$.class
$OutputPath$/$FileDirRelativeToSourcepath$
```

配置好后即可右键跳转

![image-20230113230441546](/imgs/oss/blog/vampireachao/image-20230113230441546.png)
