![](/imgs/oss/blog-img/2023-11-19-12-20-44-image.png)

![](/imgs/oss/blog-img/2023-11-19-12-36-39-image.png)

输入`IP`和用户名

![](/imgs/oss/blog-img/2023-11-19-12-37-15-image.png)

![](/imgs/oss/blog-img/2023-11-19-12-37-41-image.png)
